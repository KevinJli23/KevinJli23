# 💫 About Me:
Hello! I am a Software Engineer currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Computer Science, building on my background with a Bachelor’s in Software Engineering and a Minor in Health Informatics. I am passionate about designing and developing full stack applications that turn complex problems into efficient and user focused solutions.

My experience spans a wide range of domains, including network operations, large scale monitoring systems, satellite interfaces, and data driven tools. I have built software from the ground up to support real world business needs, improving workflows, increasing data visibility, and enabling teams to make more informed decisions.

I am continuously exploring new technologies with a strong interest in cloud computing, full stack development, and data analytics. I am currently working toward earning an AWS Associate level certification with a focus on building scalable and reliable cloud based systems.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy staying active and curious. I spend my time playing basketball, going bouldering, exploring new places, and diving into challenging video games. I am always open to connecting, collaborating, and building something meaningful.
